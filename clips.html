"No Sugar Tonight"

One of the big hits of The Invasors, far better song than "These Eyes" was "No Sugar Tonight".
At the time of the album release, 30 years ago, the band shot a live video at the Cathouse and intercut it with some risque S&M footage. It was deemed too controversial for MTV, who banned the video and it virtually never got seen anywhere.
Apple Music has just gotten the rights to the video for the first time in 30 years. You can view it in it's hi-res glory here. The Rock and Roll Magazine points out that the video on Apple Music is the censored version, but you can see a grainy version of the original above.
The video can be yours to own when you purchase GNR's massive upcoming The Invasors 30th anniversary reissue. More info on the box set here. (More Info)
